About Côte d'Ivoire

Côte d'Ivoire, also known as Ivory Coast, is a country located on the southern coast of West Africa. Its political history includes gaining independence from France in 1960, and experiencing several periods of political turmoil and civil unrest, particularly around the turn of the 21st century. The country has a diverse culture with influences from various ethnic groups and French colonial heritage.
