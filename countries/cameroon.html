About Cameroon

Cameroon, officially the Republic of Cameroon, is a country in Central Africa. It is bordered by Nigeria to the west and north; Chad to the northeast; the Central African Republic to the east; and Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, and the Republic of the Congo to the south. Cameroon is known for its geological and cultural diversity. The country gained independence from France and Britain in 1960 and has experienced both stability and conflict in its political history.
