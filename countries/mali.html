About Mali

Mali, a landlocked country in West Africa, is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. The Mali Empire, which existed from the 13th to the 16th century, was one of the largest and wealthiest empires in African history. Mali gained independence from France in 1960. Its political history has been marked by periods of stability as well as turmoil, including a series of military coups. The country faces ongoing challenges related to poverty, security, and political instability.
