About Togo

Togo is a country in West Africa bordered by Ghana to the west, Benin to the east, and Burkina Faso to the north. It extends south to the Gulf of Guinea, where its capital Lomé is located. Togo gained independence from France in 1960 and has experienced periods of political instability and military rule. Despite its challenges, Togo is known for its diverse culture and economy primarily based on agriculture.
