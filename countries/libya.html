About Libya

Libya is a country located in North Africa, bordered by the Mediterranean Sea to the north. It gained independence from Italy in 1951. Libya's modern history has been marked by the long rule of Muammar Gaddafi, who led the country from 1969 until his overthrow and death in 2011 during the Libyan Civil War. Since then, Libya has experienced ongoing conflict and political instability. The country is known for its rich cultural heritage and significant oil reserves.
