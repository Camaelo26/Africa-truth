About Ethiopia

Ethiopia, located in the Horn of Africa, is one of the oldest countries in the world and has a rich history dating back thousands of years. It is known for its archaeological sites, including the famous Lucy fossil, one of the oldest hominid skeletons ever discovered. Ethiopia was one of the few African countries to avoid colonization and has a diverse culture with over 80 ethnic groups and languages. The country is also known for its unique Orthodox Christian heritage and ancient churches carved out of rock.
