About Uganda

Uganda, officially the Republic of Uganda, is a landlocked country in East Africa. Known for its diverse landscapes that encompass the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains and immense Lake Victoria, Uganda gained independence from Britain in 1962. Its political history includes periods of military rule, civil unrest, and efforts towards democratic governance. Uganda is also renowned for its biodiversity and cultural heritage.
