About Mozambique

Mozambique is a country located in Southeast Africa bordered by the Indian Ocean to the east. It gained independence from Portugal in 1975, and its history includes a prolonged civil war from 1977 to 1992. Mozambique is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse wildlife, and natural resources. The country has made significant strides in economic development despite facing challenges such as natural disasters and political instability.
