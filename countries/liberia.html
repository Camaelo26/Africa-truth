About Liberia

Liberia, located on the West African coast, is Africa's oldest republic, founded by freed American and Caribbean slaves in 1847. Its history includes periods of significant political instability, including two civil wars between 1989 and 2003. Despite its tumultuous past, Liberia has a rich cultural heritage and is working towards political and economic recovery.
