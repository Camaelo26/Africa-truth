About Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone, located on the southwest coast of West Africa, is known for its diverse environment, which includes savannas, rainforests, and mountains. The country gained independence from the United Kingdom in 1961. Sierra Leone has experienced significant political challenges, including a brutal civil war from 1991 to 2002. Despite these challenges, it is rich in natural resources, particularly diamonds. Sierra Leone is culturally diverse, with multiple ethnic groups and languages.
