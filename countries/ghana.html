About Ghana

Ghana, located in West Africa, is known for its diverse cultures, rich history, and significant contributions to African history and politics. It was the first African country to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957. Ghana has experienced periods of political instability and military rule, but it has been a stable democracy since the early 1990s. The country is rich in natural resources, including gold and cocoa, which have been vital to its economy.
