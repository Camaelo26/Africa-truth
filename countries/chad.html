About Chad

Chad, located in north-central Africa, is a landlocked country with diverse cultures and a history of political instability. Since its independence from France in 1960, Chad has experienced numerous conflicts and changes in government. The country faces significant challenges in development, with ongoing efforts to improve the political and economic situation.
