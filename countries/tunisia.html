About Tunisia

Tunisia, officially known as the Republic of Tunisia, is a country in North Africa. It is the northernmost country on the African continent and is bordered by Algeria, Libya, and the Mediterranean Sea. Tunisia has a rich history dating back to ancient Carthage, followed by Roman, Arab, Ottoman, and French influences. The country gained independence from France in 1956 and has since developed a diverse culture with significant Arab, Berber, and Mediterranean influences.
