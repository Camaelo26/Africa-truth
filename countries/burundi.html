About Burundi

Burundi is a landlocked country in East Africa bordered by Rwanda, Tanzania, and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. It gained independence from Belgium in 1962. Burundi has faced numerous challenges including ethnic conflicts, civil wars, and political instability. Despite these challenges, Burundi has a rich cultural heritage with various traditional practices and arts.
