About Nigeria

Nigeria, located on the western coast of Africa, is the most populous country on the continent and one of the largest oil producers in the world. It gained independence from Britain in 1960. Nigeria's political history has been marked by a series of military coups and a civil war (the Biafran War) from 1967 to 1970. Despite political instability, Nigeria has a rich cultural heritage with a diverse array of ethnic groups and languages.
