About Burkina Faso

Burkina Faso, located in West Africa, is a landlocked country with a rich cultural heritage and diverse ethnic groups. It gained independence from France in 1960. The country has faced several political challenges, including coups and periods of instability. Despite these challenges, Burkina Faso remains known for its vibrant culture, music, and arts.
