About Kenya

Kenya, located on the eastern coast of Africa, is known for its diverse landscapes, wildlife, and cultural heritage. It gained independence from Britain in 1963. Kenya's political history has been characterized by a series of political and social changes, including the struggle for independence, periods of political unrest, and efforts to establish a multiparty democracy. The country is a major economic and transportation hub in the region.
