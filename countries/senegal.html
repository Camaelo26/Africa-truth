About Senegal

Senegal, located on the westernmost point of the African continent, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stable democracy. It gained independence from France in 1960 and has since been one of the most stable countries in the region. Senegal's political history is characterized by its peaceful transitions of power and strong civil society. The country is also known for its diverse cultural heritage, including music, dance, and art.
