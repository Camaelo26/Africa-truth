About Benin

Benin, officially the Republic of Benin, is a country in West Africa. It is bordered by Togo to the west, Nigeria to the east, Burkina Faso and Niger to the north, and the Atlantic Ocean to the south. Benin's political history includes gaining independence from France in 1960, followed by a period of political instability and a series of military coups. The country transitioned to a stable democracy in the 1990s. Benin has a rich cultural heritage, including the historical Kingdom of Dahomey and vibrant traditions in art and music.
