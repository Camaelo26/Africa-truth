About the Central African Republic

The Central African Republic (CAR) is a landlocked country in Central Africa. It gained independence from France in 1960. The country's history has been marked by political instability, conflicts, and coups. Despite these challenges, the Central African Republic has a rich cultural heritage, with numerous ethnic groups and languages.
