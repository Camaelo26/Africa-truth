My Mission

Welcome to the Political Map of Africa project. This project was created to help people understand the political history of their countries, particularly African countries. Our goal is to raise awareness about the political developments that have shaped our present and to inspire a better future by learning from the past.

Why This Project?

Understanding the past is crucial for comprehending the present and building a better future. By exploring the political history of African countries, we aim to provide insights into the significant events and changes that have influenced our world today.

About Me

I am Macarthur Diby,a CS student passionate about history, especially political history. This project is a reflection of my dedication to educating and informing others about the historical contexts that define our current political landscapes.
