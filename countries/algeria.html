About Algeria

Algeria is a country in North Africa on the Mediterranean coast. It is the largest country in Africa and the Arab world. Algeria gained its independence from France in 1962 after a brutal war of independence. The country has a rich cultural heritage with influences from Arab, Berber, and French cultures. Algeria's economy is heavily reliant on hydrocarbons, and it has the 16th largest oil reserves in the world.
