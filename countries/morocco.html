About Maroc

Maroc, also known as Morocco, is a country located in North Africa with a rich history and diverse cultural heritage. It gained independence from France in 1956. Morocco is known for its unique blend of Arab, Berber, and European cultural influences. The country has a constitutional monarchy and has undergone significant political and economic changes over the years.
